The course is amazing for anyone who wants to earn the diploma for angelic healing. The content is comprehensive but is still manageable in order for the students to follow through smoothly. The visuals used for the lesson were really engaging and helps the students absorb the lesson. The instructor goes in-depth about the information of each archangel and their specificities that make the students drawn to them. The course is very enlightening and teaches the students a lot about the angels. It challenges the students to go outside their comfort zone and look more towards higher dimension that they have never seen before.


The instructor teaches wonderfully and has a lot of experiences to share. She has a lot of soul and passion incorporated into her teachings. This course enables the students to be more confident in their healing practice and gives them time to practice by giving them quizzes. All the equipment needed in order to do good healing practice is readily available for the students and are not hard to find. The course deepens the relationship of the students toward the angels and understands the relationship between the angels and crystals. This is a wonderful course who wants to connect to another realm and reach another level of spirituality.

Deepens the students relationship with the angels and lets them learn more about them. This course helps the student be more spiritual and be more confident in their healing practice.
