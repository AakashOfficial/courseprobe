What is Digital Marketing ?

In simple terms Digital Marketing is the implementation of a series of techniques and tools that will allow marketers to interact with consumers through digital channels and marketplaces with the end goal to advertise and measure sales of goods and services.

The usual digital channels used by professional marketers are Paid per Click, Search Engine marketing, Video Advertising and Social Media Advertising.

Social media advertising is used not only to promote products but also to build communities around brands.

What is the profile of a Digital Marketing Professional ?

A digital marketing Professional is a person who has experience in traditional marketing and using digital tools implements strategies aimed to reinforce the offline marketing strategies.

Usually you would start your career at an Executive or Associate level then be promoted to Junior Digital Marketing Manager, Marketing Director and then VP of Marketing.

Other professionals opt for working as Account directors at media or digital Agencies where they get to direct a group of professionals working at an operational level while creating and managing campaigns for clients.

A digital marketer will know all the different KPIs and will use the tools to optimize campaigns to improve ROI and ROAS for the company is working for or clients.
